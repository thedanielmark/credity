About Fringe Messenger
DHS is a symmetric-key cryptographic system designed and developed by
  • Daniel Mark
  • Harrison Mathew
  • Sajwa Furood
DHS claims to be the world's only cryptographic system that cannot be broken even by a 'Quantum Computer.'

This system makes use of what's known as a 'BlackBox' to create a secret mapping of a single input variable to 1 of either 32 possible outcomes.
